Random Facts about Badminton

Here are some random facts about Badminton:

  • Badminton is the fastest racquet sport in the world, with shuttles traveling at speeds up to 200 mph.
  • The game of badminton originated in India over 2000 years ago.
  • The first Badminton World Championships were held in 1977.
  • Badminton made its Olympic debut as a demonstration sport in 1972 and became an official Olympic sport in 1992.
  • The longest badminton match on record lasted for 161 minutes.
